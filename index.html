Types of Grass:


Green Grass

this is the most common form of grass. it's attributes include being green, making you feel connected to nature, and one in every billion will make you float upon contact.


Blue Grass

Mostly found in Kentucky, blue grass is the most aversive form of grass as people attempt to avoid it at all costs after first contact (accept for a small subset of people we won't talk about on this page.)

Orange Grass

Orange grass also known as light red grass, is the most sacred form of grass known to man. However one of the most well kept secrets that top government officials have tried to do everything in their power to keep, is that orange grass is just yellow grass with red paint.

Black and White Grass

Finally We have black and White grass. this is rarest form of grass known to humanity and is known to bring enlightenment upon anyone that comes in contact with it. Sadly humans can no longer achieve enlightenment as black and white grass went exctinct in 1861 along with the birth of colored photography.
